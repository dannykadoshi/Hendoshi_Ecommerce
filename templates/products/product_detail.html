Not satisfied with your purchase? No problem! You have 30 days from delivery to return items for a full refund.

How to Return

  1. Contact us at returns@hendoshi.com with your order number
  2. Pack your items securely in their original packaging
  3. Include the return authorization form we'll send you
  4. Ship to: HENDOSHI Returns, [Return Address], EU

Return Conditions

  • Items must be unworn and in original condition
  • Tags must still be attached

How to Measure

  • Chest: Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal
  • Length: Measure from the highest point of the shoulder to the bottom hem
  • Sleeve: Measure from the shoulder seam to the end of the cuff

Our garments run true to size. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more relaxed fit.
