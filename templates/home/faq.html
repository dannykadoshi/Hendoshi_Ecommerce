Common Questions

Ordering & Shipping

How long does shipping take?
US orders typically arrive within 3-5 business days. International orders take 7-14 business days depending on your location.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship worldwide. International shipping costs vary by location and are calculated at checkout.
Can I track my order?
Absolutely. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. You can also check your order status in your account.
What if my order arrives damaged?
Contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We'll send a replacement or process a full refund.

Products & Sizing

What sizes do you offer?
We offer sizes from XS to 4XL. Check our size guide for detailed measurements. All items are printed on demand for quality.
Are your shirts true to size?
Our shirts run true to size. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more relaxed fit.
What material are your products made from?
Our apparel is made from high-quality, comfortable fabrics. Tees are 100% cotton, hoodies are cotton/poly blends for durability.
Do you offer custom designs?
We don't offer custom designs at this time, but we release new original designs regularly. Follow us for updates!

Returns & Exchanges

What's your return policy?
We offer 14-day returns on unworn, unwashed items in original packaging. Free return shipping for US customers.
Can I exchange my order?
Yes! Exchanges are processed the same as returns. We'll send your new items once we receive your return.
What items can't be returned?
Custom orders, worn/washed items, and items damaged due to misuse cannot be returned. See our full returns policy for details.

About HENDOSHI

What does "Wear Your Weird" mean?
It means embracing who you are, no matter how different or unconventional. Our designs celebrate the misfits, outsiders, and creative souls.
Where do your designs come from?
All designs are original HENDOSHI creations, inspired by metal culture, gothic art, gym motivation, dark humor, and surreal nightmares.
Why "No Filler. Just Killer."?
We don't add unnecessary elements to our designs or our brand. Everything we create is intentional, high-quality, and hits hard.
How often do you release new designs?
We release new drops regularly, usually 1-2 times per month. Follow our social media for the latest updates and exclusive previews.
