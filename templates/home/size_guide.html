Sizing Tips

  • Width: Measured across the chest, 1 inch below the armhole
  • Length: Measured from the highest point of the shoulder to the bottom hem
  • Sleeve Length: Measured from the center back neckline to the end of the sleeve
  • Fit: Our t-shirts run true to size. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up
  • Variations: Slight variations may occur due to printing and manufacturing processes
  • Model Sizing: Our product photos show models wearing size M for reference
