Returns & Exchanges

Hassle-free returns within 30 days.

Return Policy

We want you to love your HENDOSHI gear. If you're not completely satisfied, you can return your items within 14 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Return Eligibility

Time Frame
Items must be returned within 14 days of delivery
Condition
Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging
Tags Attached
All original tags and labels must be attached
Receipt
Valid proof of purchase required

Non-Returnable Items

  • Items damaged due to misuse or normal wear
  • Items washed, altered, or repaired
  • Custom or personalized items
  • Intimate apparel or underwear
  • Items purchased during sales or promotions (some restrictions may apply)

How to Return

1
Contact Us
Email us at returns@hendoshi.com with your order number and reason for return
2
Receive Approval
We'll provide a return authorization and shipping label
3
Pack & Ship
Pack items securely and ship using the provided label
4
Refund Processing
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after receipt

Important Information

  • Return Shipping: Free return shipping for US customers. International customers pay return shipping
  • Refunds: Processed to original payment method within 5-7 business days
  • Exchanges: Same value or higher. Additional payment required for upgrades
  • Damaged Items: Contact us immediately for damaged or defective items
  • Custom Orders: Final sale, no returns on custom or personalized items
  • Processing Time: Returns are processed within 1-2 business days of receipt
