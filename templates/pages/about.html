HENDOSHI was built for people who never fit the template and never wanted to. We live at the crossroads of heavy metal, gothic art, gym culture, dark humor, and pure creative chaos. Our designs smash skulls, lift iron, shred guitars, worship weird animals, melt reality, and laugh while doing it. This brand was never meant to be subtle. It's meant to hit hard.

What We Make

Every piece we create pulls from tattoo flash, old-school thrash metal album covers, gritty gym motivation, surreal nightmares, and sarcastic humor. Skeletons deadlift. Animals wear battle armor. Skulls glow, burn, and glitch. Nothing here is accidental. Nothing here is safe.

How We Do It

HENDOSHI is for the ones who train heavy, think deep, dress loud, and refuse to blend in. We don't follow trends. We don't soften edges. We don't add filler. We make apparel for misfits, outsiders, and people who wear their identity like armor.
